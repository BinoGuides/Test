Is whale watching better in morning or evening?

There is not a big difference between watching whales in the morning or evening. Whales are active all day. But in the evening there is a dimmer light than morning. Morning time is the most enjoyable and beautiful time for whale watching. But you need a good pair of binoculars for whale watching. If you have the best binoculars for whale watching then you can see that majestic animal.

What strength binoculars do I need for whale watching?

Before going to watch whales, dolphins and other seabirds you should take good strength binoculars with you. The best binoculars for whale watching have 7x to 10x magnification. You can afford greater strength than it’s good for you. But in my opinion 7x to 10x binoculars are best for whale watching having 42mm of objective lens diameter. These binoculars have crisp, bright and large fields of view.

How have binoculars benefited to the society?

Binoculars have many advantages in society. One of the main advantages of the binoculars is that they are cheaper than the optical telescope.
