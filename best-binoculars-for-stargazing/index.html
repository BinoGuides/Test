What is the difference between cheap and expensive binoculars?

As binoculars are optical instruments so one can decide cheap and expensive binoculars on the basis of quality of prisms and lenses that are used in the binoculars.

Cheap binoculars come with the low-quality lenses and prisms. Their magnification is low and objective and eyepiece diameter may be smaller than that of expensive binoculars. These binoculars are under budget any one can purchase them for startup.

Expensive binoculars provide high magnification power and larger objective lens diameter. The prism used in these binoculars made of high quality. These binoculars collect maximum transmitted light and provide large field of view with clearer image also sharp image. Everyone cannot purchase them because of budget limit.

To watch beautiful scene of astronomy you need some optical instruments that provide the clear or sharper image or vision to you. Can we look at the Sun and moon with Stargazing binoculars?

As every one knows the sun is the biggest star and transmit most dangerous radiations and light rays that are harmful for human eyes. You cannot look for long time towards sun even from the naked eyes. So, don’t try to look at the sun with your binoculars because binoculars collect the transmitted light and provide the images. As you already reflection of light from the sun in your binocular’s lenses can harm your eyes.

You know Moon has no source of light it only reflects the light that is emitted from the sun. Due to small percentage of light that is reflected by the moon. One can easily look towards the moon with the help of binoculars. You can look beautiful moon with the help of telescope and with your naked eyes. If it’s dangerous with naked ayes then it is also dangerous with the binoculars for stargazing.
